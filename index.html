Flagship Program

Youth Farm
Project

Ghana's average farmer is 55 years old. Youth make up only 5% of the agricultural workforce. The Youth Farm Project is our answer — providing Climate-Smart Agriculture training, securing 50 acres of farmland per district, and equipping young people with agribusiness and financial literacy skills.
