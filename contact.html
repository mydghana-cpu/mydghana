Partnerships

Partner with
MYD Ghana

We collaborate with NGOs, government agencies, development finance institutions, universities, and corporate entities to amplify impact across Northern Ghana.

Partnership opportunities include program co-design, co-funding, technical assistance, and capacity building. We are registered as dba Betterway Group and can enter formal MOU agreements.

Program Partnership
Co-design & Deliver Programs
